How to Play

  • 1

    Match 3 or More Gems – Swap tiles to form matches and earn points.

  • 2

    Activate Boosters – Use special moves like the Royal Bomb to clear tough levels.

  • 3

    Complete Quests – Achieve level goals to progress through the royal kingdom.

  • 4

    Unlock New Realms – Discover hidden areas and expand your castle.

  • 5

    Climb the Leaderboard – Compete globally and rule the kingdom of puzzles!
